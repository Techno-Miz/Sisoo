Prohibited items

General Detail on Prohibited Items
SiSO prohibits any items which are prohibited by any laws, regulations, or statutes of any federal, state, or local government of any country through which the shipment may be carried. Any items that would require SiSO to obtain a special license to transport the shipment are also prohibited. Finally, SiSO prohibits items that would damage equipment, personnel, or other packages.

* Country specific restrictions apply.
The above list is not all encompassing, please check country specific restrictions here.
If you have further questions, please contact customer service  support@SiSO.com
